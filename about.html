CompuEra was established in 1997 as an Egyptian joint-stock company with an initial authorized capital of 50 million EGP. For more than two decades in business, CompuEra continues to grow with its growing client list. The company currently takes pride in its impressive portfolio of successful projects and recurrent, happy clients.

Across every industry and business sector, our dynamic and innovative ICT Infrastructure technologies serve ICT, mission-critical, and business-critical applications on every level. Since our establishment, we’ve been on a mission to deliver excellence. At CompuEra we pride our experienced and devoted ICT professionals, our innovative and constantly evolving solutions, and the emerging technologies we incorporate into our products and services.

With our extension into the GCC region through TECHPORT, we now cover the entire Middle East and Africa with a successful track record in serving large enterprises. We also harbor a large pool of certified professionals from IBM, DELLEMC, Cisco, VMware, Microsoft and Oracle.
